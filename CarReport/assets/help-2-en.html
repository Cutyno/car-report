Consumption

To calculate the consumption, the miles are added together by a full tank of fuel to another. For this reason partial refills will only be considered with the next complete fill.

Costs

The values ​​in the cost report show an average value which is based on the entire input data. The graph displays the last couple of months/years. The trend line in the graph refers to all data again. So it may differ from the bars.

Trend

The trend corresponds to the regression line of the simple linear regression. It is calculated by the method of least squares.
